How it works

Three steps to a verdict.

No account needed. Paste your input, run the analysis, get a detailed report in seconds.

1

Submit a target

Paste a suspicious email, URL, QR code image, or raw email headers into the relevant analysis tab.

2

Run the analysis

The engine checks over 70 threat signals across WHOIS, SSL, DNS, redirect chains, content, and external APIs in parallel.

3

Get a verdict

Receive a 0–100 threat score, a risk level, specific indicators found, and guidance — exportable as a PDF report.

Features

Everything in one place.

Each module runs independently and in combination, covering angles that single-check tools miss.

📧

Email Analysis

Detects spoofed senders, brand impersonation, urgency tactics, and suspicious patterns across subject and body.

🔗

URL Intelligence

WHOIS lookup, domain age scoring, typosquatting detection, redirect chain tracing, SSL inspection, and content analysis.

📡

DNS Forensics

Inspects A, MX, NS, and TXT records. Flags suspicious nameservers, parking providers, and missing records.

📱

QR Code Scanner

Upload a QR code image to extract and immediately analyze the embedded URL for phishing indicators.

📋

Header Analyzer

Parses raw email headers to verify SPF, DKIM, and DMARC authentication. Flags spoofed routing chains.

🛡️

Threat APIs

Optional integrations with VirusTotal, Google Safe Browsing, and AbuseIPDB for enriched verdicts.

📄

PDF Reports

Export a professional forensics report for any scan — suitable for incident response documentation.

📊

History & Feed

Persistent scan history with stats, search, and a live threat feed from OpenPhish and URLhaus.

🔌

Browser Extension

Right-click any link in Chrome or Edge to instantly analyze it without leaving the page.

REST API

Integrate it into your workflow.

All features are available over HTTP. POST JSON and receive a structured verdict. No SDK required.
